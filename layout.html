03 — When to use it

Notes & gotchas

  • One gutter, one width. Every section shares --pad (side margin) and --maxw (1340px), so content edges line up down the whole page.
  • Sections fill the viewport. Under .cm-page, each content section is min-height:100vh and centers its content — nav / stat strip / footer are exempt.
  • Display headings: 2 lines max. Keep hero/section titles tight or use <br>; never let one wrap to three.
  • Spacing scale is 4-based (4·8·12·16·20·24·32·40·56·64·72·96·120); section rhythm lives at the big end via the section padding token.
  • This is the layer that makes the two brand pages (Patagonia, Lululemon) read as one system — build every new page on it.
